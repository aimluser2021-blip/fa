# HMF Chemical Enterprises App

Manage your entire chemical business in one smart app.

## Smart business management for chemical shops and wholesalers

The HMF Chemical Enterprises App helps you handle billing, inventory, customer records, and sales tracking from one place. It is built to save time, reduce manual errors, and help your business run more smoothly every day.

## Key features

- Easy and fast billing
  Create professional invoices in seconds and share them directly with customers through mobile SMS.

- Smart inventory management
  Track stock levels, monitor product movement, and get low-stock alerts before items run out.

- Sales history and reports
  Review daily, weekly, and monthly sales with clear reports that help you make better business decisions.

- Product catalog with images
  Add products with photos, pricing, and details so your inventory stays organized and easy to manage.

- Multiple payment options
  Accept payments through Cash, UPI, and QR Code for a faster and more flexible checkout experience.

- Customer management
  Save customer details, review purchase history, and build stronger business relationships.

- Fully customizable settings
  Manage menus, settings, and business controls your way.
